Child Safety Policy

btrlrn is dedicated to providing a safe, supportive environment for children. This policy outlines our commitment to child safety and the steps we take to protect our users.

1. Safety by Design

  • No public sharing of personal information or phone numbers by default.
  • Parental controls and oversight are built in.
  • All communication is monitored for safety and appropriateness.

2. Reporting Concerns

If you have any concerns about safety or inappropriate content, please contact us immediately at support@btrlrn.com.

3. Data Protection

  • We use secure, encrypted systems to store all data.
  • Access to data is strictly limited to authorized staff.

4. Parental Rights

  • Parents can request access to, correction, or deletion of their child’s data at any time.
  • We respond promptly to all parental requests and concerns.

Effective date: September 6, 2025
